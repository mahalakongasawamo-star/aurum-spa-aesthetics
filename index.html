    Frequently Asked

    Our approach

    Do I need a consultation before my first treatment?

    Every new guest begins with a private consultation with a member of our medical or spa team. It is unhurried, confidential, and designed around your goals. Only once we understand what you are seeking do we design your experience.
    Are your aesthetic treatments medically supervised?

    Yes. Every medical aesthetic treatment at Aurum is performed or directly supervised by a licensed, credentialed medical professional. Our clinical standard is non-negotiable.
    Do you offer treatments for men?

    Of course. Aurum welcomes all guests. Our aesthetic and spa menus are designed to be equally effective for men and women, and our team is experienced across every treatment we offer.
    What is the cancellation policy?

    We kindly request 24 hours' notice for any cancellations or changes. A credit card is required to hold all reservations.
    Do you offer gift cards?

    Yes. Aurum gift cards are available in any amount — the most thoughtful gift for anyone who deserves the best. Contact us to arrange one.
    Where is Aurum located?

    We are at 9500 Wilshire Blvd, Beverly Hills, CA 90212 — in the heart of Beverly Hills, steps from Rodeo Drive. Easily accessible from the 405 and 10 freeways, with valet parking available and street parking on Wilshire Boulevard.
